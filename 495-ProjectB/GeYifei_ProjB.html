Press H for help, Press T for ray tracing! JKIL to rotate the camera, and WS to move forward/backward, AD for UP/Down. Space for change Scene. Z for turn on/off headlight, X to turn on/off Jittler antialiasing. C to turn on/off area light. Arrows and ctrl and shift to move the moveable light.

The ray tracing maybe pretty solow (cost more than a minute), but if turn off the Area light source(Press C) and the Antialiasing (Press X), and set the reflection times to a small number, then it would be much faster!
